Simulation

There are over 21,000 homeless people in the city of Atlanta. Meanwhile, office buildings across the city sit empty every night, often with lights and air conditioning left on, while thousands sleep on the streets. What if these spaces could become temporary homeless shelters, providing a safe place for the homeless at very low cost? How practical would such a project be? What risks would it involve?
